# Cyberpunk Cityscape Pixel Art

**What:** A 64x64 pixel art depiction of a futuristic cyberpunk cityscape at night.

**Why:** This piece represents the vibrant, technology-driven ecosystem that FreelanceFlow enables — where creative freelancers and innovative clients connect to build the future together. The neon colors symbolize energy and opportunity, while the structured pixel grid reflects the precision and craftsmanship that goes into every project on the platform.

## Details
- **Canvas Size:** 64x64 pixels
- **Format:** PNG
- **Colors:** Neon cyan, magenta, purple, and deep blues with star accents
- **Style:** Original pixel art, hand-placed pixels with dithering techniques
- **Theme:** Cyberpunk / Futuristic Urban Landscape
