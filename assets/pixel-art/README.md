# Pixel Art: Cyberpunk Cityscape

**What:** A 64x64 pixel art depiction of a futuristic cyberpunk cityscape at night, featuring neon-lit skyscrapers, holographic billboards, and a glowing moon reflected in rain-slicked streets.

**Why:** I chose this theme because cyberpunk aesthetics perfectly blend the technical precision of pixel art with vibrant, imaginative creativity. The contrast between dark shadows and bright neon colors creates visual depth that pushes the boundaries of what can be expressed with limited pixels, demonstrating high creative thinking in maximizing the medium's constraints.

**Details:**
- Canvas size: 64x64 pixels
- Color palette: 16 colors with emphasis on neon pinks, cyans, and deep purples
- Style: Retro-futuristic with dithering techniques for atmospheric perspective

Created with original pixel-by-pixel placement.
