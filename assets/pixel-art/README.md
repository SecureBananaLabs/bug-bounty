# Pixel Art: Cyberpunk City

**What:** A 64x64 pixel art depiction of a futuristic cyberpunk cityscape at night.

**Why:** I chose this theme because cyberpunk aesthetics blend vibrant neon colors with moody atmospheric elements, which makes for visually striking pixel art. The contrast between dark silhouettes and glowing lights showcases the medium well, and the futuristic cityscape allows for creative use of limited pixels to suggest depth and detail.

## Details
- Canvas size: 64x64 pixels
- Format: PNG
- Colors: Limited palette with neon pinks, cyans, and deep blues/purples
- Style: Retro-futuristic pixel art with dithering for atmospheric effects
