# Pixel Art: Cyberpunk City

**What:** A 64x64 pixel art depiction of a futuristic cyberpunk cityscape at night.

**Why:** I chose this theme because cyberpunk aesthetics blend vibrant neon colors with moody atmospheres, which creates a striking visual contrast that works beautifully in pixel art. The limited color palette and small canvas size challenge the artist to convey depth and emotion through careful placement of each pixel, making it a perfect exercise in creative thinking and pixel precision.

## Details
- Canvas size: 64x64 pixels
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Color palette: Neon pinks, cyans, and purples against deep blues and blacks
