# Pixel Art: Cyberpunk City

**What:** A 64x64 pixel art depiction of a futuristic cyberpunk cityscape at night.

**Why:** I chose this theme because cyberpunk aesthetics blend vibrant neon colors with moody atmospheres, which makes for striking and recognizable pixel art. The contrast between dark buildings and glowing windows/signs creates visual depth even at small resolutions, showcasing how pixel art can convey complex scenes through careful color choice and deliberate placement of light.

## Details
- **Canvas size:** 64x64 pixels
- **Format:** PNG
- **Colors:** Limited palette with neon pinks, cyans, and deep blues/purples
- **Style:** Retro-futuristic cityscape with towering buildings and holographic billboards
