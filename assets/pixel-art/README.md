# Cosmic Cat Pixel Art

**What:** A cosmic cat floating in space, gazing at the stars with wonder.

**Why:** I chose this theme because cats are naturally curious creatures, and combining that curiosity with the infinite mystery of space creates a powerful metaphor for exploration and imagination. The neon color palette against the dark void evokes the retro-futuristic aesthetic of classic pixel art while feeling fresh and dreamlike. This piece represents the joy of looking up at the night sky and letting your mind wander.

- Canvas size: 64x64 pixels
