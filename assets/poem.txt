The Code Weaver

In circuits deep where silence hums,
A weaver of logic quietly comes.
With keystrokes light on glass and light,
He builds the world through endless night.

From Goma's dust to global stage,
His code leaps off the digital page.
No laptop, only phone in hand,
Yet crafts the tools that understand.

Each bug a beast to slay with grace,
Each bounty won, a saving grace.
For those who doubt the will of one,
Behold the work that's just begun.

So let the bits and bytes attest,
The open source that won't rest.
A developer, fierce and true,
The code weaver — Bakome, you.
