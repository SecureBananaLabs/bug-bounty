# The Developer's Journey

*A technical poem about the life of a software developer*

---

In the beginning, there was the prompt,
A blinking cursor, dark and damp,
With fingers poised above the keys,
We write the code that shapes our dreams.

---

`git init` — a sacred rite,
The first commit in the night,
`hello world` in simple light,
A developer is born tonight.

---

Through loops and branches we traverse,
Debugging feels like blessing, not a curse,
Stack Overflow our guiding star,
We search for answers near and far.

---

The pull request, a bridge we build,
From fork to main, with care and skill,
Review comments, both sharp and kind,
Improving what we leave behind.

---

Open source, a gift we share,
With strangers who become friends rare,
Contributing from every shore,
Together building something more.

---

In Docker containers, we deploy,
Kubernetes brings us joy,
CI/CD, the pipeline flows,
From commit to prod, the codebase grows.

---

Late night pushes, coffee cold,
Bugs that make us feel old,
But when the tests all pass with green,
The sweetest sight we've ever seen.

---

From `npm install` to `npm start`,
Each package plays its vital part,
The node_modules, deep and vast,
A dependency tree built to last.

---

In terminal, we type with grace,
`git commit -m "fixed the base"`,
A message clear, a purpose true,
For future devs to build upon, too.

---

The README tells the story well,
Of how to build, of how to dwell,
In this codebase, wild and free,
A developer's community.

---

So here's to those who code at night,
Who debug until the morning light,
Who open source with heart and soul,
You make the broken systems whole.

---

*Written with ❤️ by a developer, for developers*

---

## About This Poem

This poem captures the journey of a software developer through the lens of modern development practices. It touches on:

- The ritual of starting a new project (`git init`)
- The collaborative nature of open source
- The tools we use daily (Git, Docker, npm)
- The joy of debugging and testing
- The community that makes it all possible

Each stanza represents a different aspect of the developer experience, from the first line of code to the shared joy of contributing to open source.
