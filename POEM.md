# The Flow of Code and Coin

## A Poem for FreelanceFlow

In circuits born of human thought,
Where skill and need are softly wrought,
A marketplace of dreams takes shape,
No office walls, no gilded drape.

The coder weaves at midnight's edge,
While clients wake and scan the pledge,
Of proposals sent through wire and light,
Connecting dark to morning bright.

---

What currency beyond the bill?
The trust we build, the fulfilled will,
Of projects launched from screen to screen,
Where once was void, now something's seen.

The database remembers all,
Each message sent, each rise and fall,
Of ratings given, stars aligned,
In Prisma's schema, well-defined.

---

So here's to flows that freelance may find,
To APIs graceful, well-designed,
To every pull request that's made,
And every debt that code has paid.

May Stripe process what hands create,
May TypeScript guard our shared estate,
In monorepo's branching tree,
We build, we ship, we flow—we're free.

---
*Written for the FreelanceFlow project, 2025*
