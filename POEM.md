# Harbor Manifest

At FreelanceFlow the first crate is a promise,
measured in line items, sealed against the rain;
scope is the ink on a harbor manifest,
what leaves the dock should match what comes again.

Then evidence arrives in tagged containers:
screens, traces, tests, each pallet checked by hand;
proof is not thunder but labeled cargo,
stacked where both shores can read and understand.

Review lifts lanterns over midnight water,
counting the bolts, the timestamps, and the seams;
a patient tide decides what safely crosses
before the route is stamped for open seas.

When all is cleared, the harbor stays unspectacular:
no hidden hand, no fog that hides the freight;
the shipment moves, the worker sees the payout,
and trust is moored because the books ran straight.
