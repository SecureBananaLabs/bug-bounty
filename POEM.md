# Ledger of Small Bright Work

In the morning queue, a brief request glows,
a task with its sleeves rolled neatly high.
Someone brings a question, someone brings a craft,
and the quiet marketplace learns how to reply.

The dashboard hums with invoices and names,
with offers, drafts, and messages in flight.
Trust is not thunder; it is a handled thing,
checked line by line until the numbers sit right.

So build the bridge where skill can meet need,
where finished work has somewhere fair to land.
A platform earns its keep in simple ways:
clear doors, clear records, and an open hand.
