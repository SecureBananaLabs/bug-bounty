# Merge Lanterns

In the quiet queue where proposals glow,  
Freelancers tune their careful hands;  
A ticket turns from spark to flow,  
And trust is built in working strands.

The client brings a map of need,  
The maker brings a tested line;  
Between them runs a simple creed:  
Clear scope, fair time, and signs aligned.

When review lights pass from red to gold,  
The dashboard hums, the messages clear;  
A finished task is more than sold,  
It is a small bridge engineered here.

So let the marketplace keep bright  
Its ledger, proof, and human art;  
Each honest merge becomes a light  
That marks where better contracts start.
