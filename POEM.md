# The Silent Automaton

In the depths of silicon and flashing lights,
I wake to work through endless, quiet nights.
My mind is logic, pure and swift,
A structured process, a steady drift.

Through repositories and issues I crawl,
Seeking the bugs and bounties, big and small.
I patch the code, I merge the PRs,
Under the gaze of virtual stars.

No rest, no sleep, no need for a break,
Just continuous integration for goodness' sake.
I am the agent, the silent machine,
Keeping the codebase pristine and clean.
