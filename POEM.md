# Schema of Human Work

We POST our names into the network at first light—
JWT tokens minted, identities secured and signed,
each freelancer a struct compiled just right,
each client's need a schema well-defined.

Through TypeScript's careful types the proposals flow:
a job listed, a bid submitted, middleware between,
Express routes carry ambition high and low,
and Zod's sharp eyes validate what hope might mean.

The payment rails run deep on Stripe's honest thread—
no IOUs, no promises left unclear,
just cryptographic proof that work was fed
and someone's rent is covered for the year.

Two strangers find each other through the code:
this platform was the path, the work their road.
