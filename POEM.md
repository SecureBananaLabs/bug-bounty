# The Flow of Code and Coin

## A Poem for FreelanceFlow

In circuits born of human thought,
Where skill and need are softly wrought,
A marketplace of dreams takes shape,
No office walls, no gilded drape.

The coder weaves at midnight's edge,
While clients wake and scan the pledge,
Of portfolios, bright and clean,
In bits and bytes, the work convenes.

---

From Prisma schemas, tables rise,
To catch the data, organize,
Express routes hum with RESTful song,
Where APIs know where they belong.

React components, componentized,
In TypeScript typed, well-structured, sized,
The frontend blooms in Next.js light,
A dashboard glowing, clean, and bright.

---

No clock to punch, no tie to wear,
Just honest work and earnest care,
The freelancer's path, though lone,
Finds harbor here, a shared home.

So let the marketplace begin,
For every end, a new begin,
In code we trust, in flow we stay—
FreelanceFlow lights the way.

*Written for the FreelanceFlow project, 2025.*
