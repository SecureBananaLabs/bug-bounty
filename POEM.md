# Checksums at Dawn

At dawn we read the logs like weathered charts,  
Each warning pulse a buoy in shifting seas;  
We map the fault lines through a system's heart,  
Then chart a safer route through latent keys.

A parser bends; a boundary forgets its name,  
An unchecked branch invites the dark inside;  
We tighten every seam of trust in flame,  
And watch bad assumptions lose their place to hide.

No silver spells, just patient, test-lit art:  
Small proofs, clean diffs, and comments kept precise;  
A brittle module learns a stronger part,  
As fragile guesses harden into twice-checked ice.

By dusk the build is quiet, clear, and plain;  
The alerting hush is not an empty void.  
It's earned: a thousand tiny acts that train  
The code to fail with grace, not be destroyed.
