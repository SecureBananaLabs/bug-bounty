# Quiet Signals

In the hush behind the login gate,  
a packet taps the glass, then waits;  
the console keeps its patient light,  
counting risk through lines of night.

A header leans, a secret slips,  
a checksum trembles at the tips;  
we trace the fault from spark to source,  
then turn the breach from flood to course.

No trumpet sounds when trust is patched,  
no banner marks the thread we matched;  
only the logs, once sharp and red,  
fade back to green where warnings bled.

So build the shield in careful parts,  
with tests for doors and guarded starts;  
the safest code is rarely loud,  
but leaves no shadow in the cloud.
