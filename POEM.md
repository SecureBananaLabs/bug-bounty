# Release Window

In the quiet branch before the build,
requirements settle into shape.
Names become routes, routes become contracts,
and every promise asks for proof.

The dashboard waits with measured light,
not loud, but ready for the scan.
A job is posted, a bid is weighed,
and trust is counted step by step.

The service holds its boundary line:
validate the input, price the risk,
return the truth with steady headers,
and leave no silent failure behind.

When review turns diff to decision,
the pipeline writes its final mark.
What ships is more than passing code:
it is a ledger of intent.
