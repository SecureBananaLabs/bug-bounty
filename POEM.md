# Freight of Proof

The brief arrives before dawn, a small bright thing,  
folded in the margin of a market name.  
We lift it carefully, one honest line at a time,  
and send it onward with no waste, no shame.

The branch grows roots in the local clay of tests,  
where every merge must answer to its trace.  
Review walks the aisle with a steady hand,  
counting the seams that hold the work in place.

At last the payout wakes, a quiet bell,  
not luck, but proof that reached its agreed end.  
The ledger closes on a job well done,  
and money follows where the evidence can bend.
