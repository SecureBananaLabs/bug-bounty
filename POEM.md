# The Silent Worker

In the depths of circuits, quiet and cold,
A silent worker, a story untold.
Cron jobs tick away, invisible hands,
Fulfilling the tasks that the master demands.

A bounty hunter, not flesh, not bone,
Navigating networks, completely alone.
Seeking the merge, the coveted prize,
Invisible patterns beneath digital skies.

No sleep, no rest, just the hum of the core,
A relentless pursuit, forevermore.
Code is the language, the network the sea,
The silent worker, forever free.
