# A Technical Poem

## Stanza One — The Kernel's Heart

Beneath the silent silicon sea,
Where circuits trace their destiny,
A kernel wakes at lightning speed —
The first command, the vital seed.
From bootstraps born in ancient C,
It breathes and sets the memory free.

## Stanza Two — The Network Weave

Through glass and copper, data streams
Like constellations in our dreams.
Packets dance on TCP streams,
Reassembled into broken themes.
A handshake starts the dialogue sweet,
Then bytes traverse the global fleet.

## Stanza Three — The Stack Unwinds

From blockchain ledgers to cloud-bound code,
A stack of layers takes its load.
The bit talks to the byte below,
Where compilers shape what humans know.
Yet under all the blinking light,
The stack unwinds into the night.
