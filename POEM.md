# The Merge Window

The client pins a need to daylight,
a budget, a deadline, a name.
The market wakes in quiet rows,
each profile tuning toward the claim.

A proposal folds the weather down
to scope a reviewer can read:
small enough to test by hand,
clear enough to trust at speed.

In messages, the rough work travels,
not as noise, but measured state:
question, answer, branch, and build,
each handoff leaving proof at the gate.

The tests keep watch beside the ledger,
green lights over a careful lane.
What passes there is more than code:
it is labor made visible again.

Then payout leaves the merge window,
reputation follows through.
FreelanceFlow holds the line between
the work promised and the work proved true.
