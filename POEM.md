# Ledger at Compile Time

A route wakes up in morning light,
with payloads shaped and headers clean;
the tests walk first through doubtful night,
and mark the paths no eye has seen.

A worker sends a careful bid,
a client reads the trace we leave;
each commit keeps the promise hid
inside the systems we believe.

The invoice waits beyond the merge,
the logs remember every turn;
from small defects, strong products surge,
and patient builders ship and learn.

So let the pipeline hold its line,
let review refine the flame;
we turn rough work to trusted design,
and sign each fix with a human name.
