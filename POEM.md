# The Silent Weaver

In valleys woven not of earth but logic, deep and cold,
Where threads of light through silicon intricately unfold,
The weaver works in shadows, speaking only to the core,
A quiet crafter shaping worlds behind an iron door.

A stray condition, errant loop, or unhandled routine,
Can crumble all the architectures built upon the screen.
But steady hands will trace the fault and mend the broken string,
Until the frozen mechanisms spark to life and sing.

So honor to the unseen hands that balance load and space,
That catch the falling exceptions and hold them all in grace.
For every seamless surface that the waking world can see,
Relies upon the weaver's craft and quiet mastery.
