# The Review Before Dawn

We enter where the status lights are low,
past quiet forms and routes that never sleep;
the payload knocks, the hidden branches show.

A failing test begins its narrow sweep,
it names the edge the happy path ignored;
one brittle guard wakes up and learns to keep.

The diff is small, but underneath the board
a chain of trust is tightened link by link;
no banner sounds, no victory is stored.

We read the logs until the patterns blink,
then leave a cleaner trail than what we found;
the safest code is often built in ink.

At dawn the merge queue hums without a sound,
the bug is gone, the reason still remains;
a bounty paid for doubt that held its ground.
