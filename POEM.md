# The Switchyard Keeps the Work

At dawn the board wakes quiet,
not with noise, but rows of light;
a client pins a need in place,
a freelancer trims the route just right.

Scope rolls in on narrow rails,
each promise tagged, each edge made plain;
the work moves only where trust says go,
past signal glass and careful rain.

Proof arrives in measured packets,
screens and notes and passing tests;
reviewers lean above the map,
and mark the safest merge from the rest.

Payment waits behind a golden switch,
not hidden, not hurried, not guessed;
it opens when the record holds,
when shipped work has answered the request.

So FreelanceFlow keeps its evening lamp,
for hands that build and teams that choose;
contracts become a steady track,
and finished work finds clean reviews.
