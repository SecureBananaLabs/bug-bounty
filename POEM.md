# The Quiet Handoff

In FreelanceFlow the brief arrives,
a small map pinned to working light;
the client names the shape of done,
the builder turns that shape to sight.

No trumpet sounds for clean commits,
just checks that pass and notes that stay;
each message keeps the doorway clear
so scope and craft can share a day.

The budget waits behind the glass,
not locked away, not loosely thrown;
it marks the trust between two screens
until the finished work is known.

A patch becomes a steady proof,
a review becomes a careful key;
the merge queue hums, the ledger wakes,
and effort finds its currency.

So let the marketplace be plain:
fair terms, sharp tests, a visible trail;
where promises become release,
and honest handoffs rarely fail.
