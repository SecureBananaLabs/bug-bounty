# Proof Window

The brief arrives with its edges clean,
a small blue flame in the morning queue;
FreelanceFlow opens the work between
the promise made and the proof made true.

Escrow waits like a sealed glass room,
holding the budget in patient light;
scope becomes less guess and gloom,
more checklist, commit, and measurable right.

Messages keep the timeline clear,
questions pinned where the answers belong;
each milestone turns from hope to gear,
each handoff clicks into something strong.

Tests run quiet across the page,
green marks gathering under review;
the diff is small, the intent is staged,
and trust has evidence it can use.

When approval lands, the ledger sings,
not loudly, but with a steadier grace:
good work travels on visible strings,
and payment finds its rightful place.
