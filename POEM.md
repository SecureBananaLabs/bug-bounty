# Verified Harbor

At FreelanceFlow, the briefs come in,
Like ships beneath a morning sky;
Each scope is trimmed, each task begins,
Where honest terms and talents tie.

The client lights a careful flame,
The maker shapes it line by line;
Proof bears the work, review names claim,
And small green checks begin to shine.

No whisper buys a hidden gate,
No handshake drifts beyond the view;
The escrow clock is calm and straight,
It keeps the promised harbor true.

When merge bells ring across the board,
The payout tide can find its course;
A finished craft, a clean reward,
Both move from trust, not fear or force.

So build the bridge, then show the span,
Let evidence and payment rhyme;
For every client, every hand,
FreelanceFlow keeps faithful time.
