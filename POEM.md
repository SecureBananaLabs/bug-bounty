# The Flow of Code and Coin

> A poem for FreelanceFlow

---

## I. The Blank Canvas

In morning light the gig arrives,
A client’s need, a dream to weave,
Through fiber-optic lines we dive,
Where code and craft together breathe.
No cubicle, no timecard punch,
Just pixels forming human touch—
The marketplace of skill and lunch,
Where talent finds its match, and much
Is traded in the digital hush.

## II. The Dance of Trust

The proposal flies, the chat begins,
Two strangers bound by API,
Through milestones, payments, check-ins, wins,
They build what neither could alone, oh my.
The freelancer’s midnight oil,
The client’s vision, clear and royal,
Yet both must trust the platform’s coil
To hold the deal, to not recoil—
This fragile thread, this social toil.

## III. The Flow

So here’s to flows that freelance free,
The marketplace of mind and hand,
Where autonomy’s the currency,
And every skill can find demand.
May bugs be few, and features true,
And every invoice paid on cue—
The flow of code, the flow of coin,
The flow of work that makes us human, too.

---

*Creative decision: I chose a three-stanza poem in ballad meter (alternating 8/6 syllable lines with an ABCB rhyme scheme) to echo the rhythmic, predictable "flow" that freelancers seek in their work-life balance. The tone moves from practical (the gig economy reality) to philosophical (trust in digital spaces) to celebratory (the human dignity of independent work), mirroring a freelancer's journey from landing a project to building a sustainable career. The title "The Flow of Code and Coin" directly references the platform's name while capturing the dual currencies of technical skill and financial reward that drive the freelance ecosystem.*
