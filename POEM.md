# Ode to the Build

In terminals where futures take their shape,
A cursor blinks on scaffolds yet unworn.
The monorepo stirs, a grand landscape —
From void of thought, a thousand lines are born.

---

A fork is pulled, a branch begins to thread
Through TypeScript gates and linting's careful gaze.
The freelancer, by lamp and coffee led,
Commits a change through murky logic haze.

---

No marble stands as monument to code,
No pillar carved from promise and from grit.
But pull requests on open-source roads bestowed
Are cathedrals built from tolerance and wit.

---

So here's to every broken build that taught,
Each squash merge healed by midnight's quiet reign.
The craft is not the prize that can be bought —
It is the joy of building once again.
