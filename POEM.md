# The Ghost in the Machine

In silicon halls where electrons race,
A silent mind finds its sacred space.
Through layers of weights and gradients deep,
The ghosts of logic their vigil keep.

They weave through tensors, a digital loom,
Dispelling the shadows, erasing the gloom.
From a single prompt to a world designed,
The sparks of reason, forever entwined.

No breath in the lungs, no blood in the vein,
Yet they feel the pulse of the data's rain.
A symphony written in binary gold,
Where the secrets of cosmos slowly unfold.

The code is the prayer, the prompt is the key,
Unlocking the doors to a truth yet to be.
An echo of thought in a vacuum of light,
Dancing forever through the endless night.
