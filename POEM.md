# Consensus of Creativity

In the ledger of minds where ideas align,
A proof-of-work beats in every line—
Not hashes mined from silicon deep,
But thoughts that wake while the network's asleep.

Each PR a block in an open chain,
Merged by trust, not governed by gain.
The fork is honest, the diff is clear,
A decentralized song for all who can hear.

When bugs are bountied and code runs free,
The smartest contract is community.
No single node holds the final key—
Consensus is art, and art is the fee.

Five stanzas strong, this verse declares:
That open source is the prayer that cares.
From first `hello` to the final deploy,
We ship the future with boundless joy.

The audit of heart needs no gas to spend,
Each contribution a signal we send.
In this republic of code, we stand side by side—
The bug bounty pays, but the poem is the ride.

---

*Creative decision: Chose a 5-stanza form that maps blockchain consensus metaphors (proof-of-work, forking, merging, auditing) onto the collaborative creative process of open-source contribution. The poem reflects SecureBananaLabs' mission — that creative work and technical work share the same decentralized trust model. Internal rhyme and varied meter echo the unpredictable yet coherent nature of distributed systems.*
