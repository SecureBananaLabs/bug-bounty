# The Flow of Code and Coin

## A Poem for FreelanceFlow

---

In circuits deep where data streams align,
A marketplace of skill and will takes shape,
Where client dreams and coder crafts combine,
And every keystroke holds a fair escape.
No cubicle, no clock to bind the hand,
Just flowing work through fiber, screen, and cloud,
The freelancer across the world may stand,
And sell their craft to those who seek it proud.

---

The database hums with Prisma-sculpted grace,
Each schema line a promise kept in type,
From auth to payment, every route in place,
The API stands vigilant and ripe.
React components bloom like spring's first light,
On Next.js shores where modern frontends dwell,
While Express beneath keeps watch through day and night,
And WebSocket whispers weave their patient spell.

---

So here's to bugs that teach us how to grow,
To pull requests that bridge the distant night,
To monorepos where our best selves show,
And open source that sets the code aright.
May every bounty hunter find their mark,
May every flow of freelance find its way,
From README's start to deployment's spark,
We build tomorrow, line by line, today.

---
