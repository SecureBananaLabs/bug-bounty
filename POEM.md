# The Review Ledger

A report arrives with a careful hand,
not loud with blame, not thin with fear;
it names the route, the broken strand,
and leaves a trail the next mind can clear.

A fixture wakes in the runner's light,
small facts aligned in a narrow frame;
the failing case turns red, then right,
and proves the fix before the claim.

The reviewer reads where trust is made,
through changed lines, logs, and measured scope;
no hidden leap, no borrowed shade,
just evidence giving the patch its hope.

The queue grows quiet when checks agree,
the branch is ready, the record plain;
from issue to merge, from risk to fee,
good work becomes a safer chain.

So keep the ledger clean and bright,
with proofs that travel line by line;
each bounty paid for careful sight,
each fix a small and public sign.
