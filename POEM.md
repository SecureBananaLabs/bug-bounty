# The Architect's Reverie

Lines of logic, dreams unfurled,
A canvas built in silicon world.
Where brackets hold the weight of thought,
And every bug a lesson taught.

The terminal hums a quiet song,
Of algorithms marching strong.
Through nested loops and endless night,
A developer brings code to light.

Not just function, not just grace,
But human purpose leaves its trace.
In every commit, every deploy,
A small world built with quiet joy.

So let the pixels paint the screen,
And servers process what's unseen.
For in this craft of if and when,
We build the future, time and again.
