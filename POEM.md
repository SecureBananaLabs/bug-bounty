# Ledger Handshake

The brief arrives with measured light,
a scope drawn clean in ink;
each promise finds a numbered line,
each question earns its link.

A builder answers with a plan,
not thunder, but a trace:
small proofs that travel commit by commit
until the work has shape.

The client reads the passing tests,
the notes, the edge cases known;
review becomes a steady door
where hidden risk is shown.

Escrow waits without a boast,
a quiet, counted rail;
when acceptance meets the merged request,
the payout leaves a trail.

So FreelanceFlow keeps work in view,
from offer through release:
a ledger made of honest hands,
and contracts closed in peace.
