# Ledger Moon

By evening glass the offers rise,
Soft lanterns on a crowded screen;
A client writes where purpose lies,
A maker answers, sharp and clean.

The milestones hum, the messages glow,
Each promise weighed before it flies;
Through tested proof the doubts run low,
And honest work outlives disguise.

Reviewers read with patient eyes,
They weigh the craft, not boast or name;
If flaws appear, the notes are wise,
If craft rings true, they sign the claim.

Then escrow opens like a bloom,
And payout crosses silver wire;
Beneath the quiet ledger moon,
Good labor warms a common fire.

So let the marketplace be bright,
Where trust is earned and records stay;
FreelanceFlow keeps work in sight,
And sends fair wages on their way.
