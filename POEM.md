# The Code That Binds

In circuits deep where logic flows,
A digital garden where no one knows
The bugs that hide in silent code,
Waiting for the careless load.

The freelancer types with steady hand,
Building platforms across the land,
Where payments flow and messages fly,
Beneath the watch of the admin'\''s eye.

But woe to those who forget the guard,
Who leave the authentication scarred,
For when the routes are left unbound,
The secrets of users shall hit the ground.

So write your middleware, test your auth,
Before you deploy and feel the wrath,
For in this bounty of code and trust,
Security is the one thing you must not bust.

*— A technical ode to SecureBananaLabs, where every PR is a step toward safer code.*
