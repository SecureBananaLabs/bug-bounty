# The Switchboard at Close of Review

A job card wakes beneath the morning glass,
its budget bright, its scope pinned clean and small;
the queue hums softly as proposals pass,
each name a wire waiting for the call.

Across the pane, a message finds its route,
from client note to freelancer reply;
no trumpet sounds, no fireworks shoot,
just trust made legible line by line.

The dashboards keep their lanterns trimmed and low,
with tests for gates and records for the trail;
what changed, who saw, which promises still glow,
all logged before the final hand can sail.

Then review arrives, a quiet measured thing,
not loud applause, but evidence made plain;
the work stands up, the payment rails can sing,
and finished effort crosses back again.

So FreelanceFlow keeps circuits warm at night:
brief, bid, proof, receipt, and rating true;
a marketplace of signals, set alight,
where careful hands make honest work move through.
