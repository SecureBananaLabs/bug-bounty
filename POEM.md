# FreelanceFlow

## A Poem for the Freelance Marketplace

### Stanza I — The Marketplace

In the city of code, where the freelancers roam,
A marketplace rises, a digital home.
No longer bound by the office's cold light,
FreelanceFlow glimmers—connect, create, write.

### Stanza II — The Exchange

Clients post dreams in the glow of their screens,
Freelancers answer with skill-sets unseen.
From landing page to the billing's最后 tick,
The marketplace hums—transaction, not trick.

### Stanza III — The Architecture

Next.js sings on the frontend so bright,
Express routes whisper through layers of night.
Prisma draws schemas like rivers through stone,
Zod validates every request that has flown.

### Stanza IV — The Spirit

Like Hermés who linked gods and mortals with ease,
This platform connects where and whene'er we please.
From proposal to payment, from hire to star—
FreelanceFlow builds what the gig economy are.

---

*Written for the FreelanceFlow monorepo — celebrating the invisible infrastructure of modern work: the contracts, the code, and the countless hours developers pour into platforms we use without thinking. The creative choice was to frame FreelanceFlow through a mythological lens (Hermés as the messenger), drawing a parallel between ancient trade routes and modern gig economies.*
