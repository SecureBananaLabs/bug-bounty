# The Pressroom at Closing Time

FreelanceFlow sorts the day's brief type,
small metal promises set line by line:
client need, freelancer craft,
price and scope locked square in the chase.

Messages pass like fresh galley sheets,
margin notes bright under a reading lamp.
A bug is not a scandal there,
only a wrong letter asking for daylight.

The test press turns without applause.
It pulls one page, then another,
until every API handoff, invoice, and review
sits where the contract said it would.

At closing, escrow releases its quiet ink.
The worker signs off, the client reads twice,
and trust leaves the shop as a clean receipt,
warm from the press, exact to the cent.
