# The Code Weaver's Dream

In the quiet hours when the world sleeps deep,
A coder sits where shadows creep,
Lines of logic, patterns bright,
Weaving dreams through the digital night.

The compiler hums its gentle song,
Where functions dance and loops belong,
Each variable a thread of thought,
In battles fought and lessons taught.

Open source, the garden grows,
Where every contribution shows,
From tiny seeds of `hello world`,
To flags of progress, bright unfurled.

The terminal glows with amber light,
As git commits push through the night,
Branch by branch, we build our way,
Merging hope into the day.

Debug the fears, refactor the pain,
Let algorithms heal the strain,
For in this code, we find our art—
The human beat inside the heart.

So raise a glass to those who dare,
To write the code beyond compare,
In ones and zeros, dreams take flight,
The weaver works through endless night.
