# The Accountable Handshake

A brief arrives with measured edges,
its scope laid out in quiet lines;
the work begins where doubt is named,
and every promise leaves a sign.

The builder lifts a proof from silence,
not polished smoke, not borrowed flame;
each test becomes a small receipt,
each commit learns to state its name.

Reviewers walk the change like ledger,
from opened thread to closing gate;
they weigh the craft, the risk, the record,
then let the useful parts update.

When merge light settles on the branch,
the payout path can find its way;
trust is not magic in the marketplace,
just work made visible enough to pay.

So reputation grows by handoff,
by honest notes and finished threads;
the strongest systems keep their word
in all the human steps they shed.
