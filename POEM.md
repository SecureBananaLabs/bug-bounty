# Signal Garden

In quiet wires where small lights grow,
A marketplace learns trust by name;
Each honest task begins to glow,
And every promise guards the flame.

A builder brings a careful hand,
A client brings a hopeful brief;
Together they can shape a land
Where work is paid with less grief.

So let the ledger bloom with care,
Let messages arrive on time;
Let fair rewards move through the air,
And turn good craft into a rhyme.
