# The Flow of Code and Coin

> A poem for FreelanceFlow

---

In circuits where the data streams at night,  
A marketplace of skill takes digital flight—  
No cubicle, no boss, no fixed address,  
Just talent mapped to need, and nothing less.

The client posts a dream in structured fields,  
The freelancer replies with what she wields:  
A proposal crafted, sharp as any knife,  
To trade her hours for the coin of life.

---

Through Prisma schemas, relationships are born,  
Users, jobs, and messages sworn—  
Each table holds a story, row by row,  
Of trust built slow, or trust that had to grow.

The API hums with layered, clean design,  
Controllers, services, each in line,  
Zod validates what human hands have typed,  
While middleware keeps chaos far and wide.

---

So here's to flows that free us from the gate,  
To code that connects, to platforms that create—  
Not just a gig, but dignity of trade,  
In bits and bytes, new economies are made.

FreelanceFlow, you bridge the wide world's art,  
One pull request, one heartbeat, one small part.

---
