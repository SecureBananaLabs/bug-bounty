# The Flow of Code and Coin

## A Poem for FreelanceFlow

In circuits born of human thought,
Where skill and need are softly wrought,
A marketplace of dreams takes shape,
No office walls, no fixed landscape.

The client posts their hopes at dawn,
A project brief, a vision drawn;
The freelancer, with tools in hand,
Builds castles in the digital land.

Through messages that cross the wire,
Ideas spark, and passions fire,
Proposals flow like rivers wide,
Where talent meets the turning tide.

From landing page to dashboard deep,
Where notifications never sleep,
The API hums, steady, strong,
A silent, ever-present song.

In Stripe we trust, the coin to pass,
For work well done, no looking glass,
Just honest code and honest pay,
The freelance way, the modern way.

So here's to flows that freely run,
To projects lost and battles won,
To every dev who codes at night—
You are the FreelanceFlow's true light.

---

*Creative decision: I chose a traditional ballad meter with an optimistic, communal tone to reflect 
the collaborative spirit of freelance marketplaces. The rhyme scheme (ABCB) evokes the rhythmic, 
reliable "flow" suggested by the product name, while celebrating both the technical architecture 
and the human connections it enables.*
