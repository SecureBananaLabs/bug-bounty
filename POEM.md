# The Calibration Table

Before the contract learns a name,
we lay the scope beneath a lamp:
not large enough to swallow doubt,
not small enough to hide a gap.

A bidder brings a careful hand,
a client brings the reason why;
between them, fields of evidence
turn guesswork into something dry.

The issue opens like a gauge,
red when promise outruns proof,
green when tests and notes agree
and reviewers find a standing roof.

No trumpet sounds for honest work,
just comments clear, a branch made plain;
the merge is not a magic door,
it is a record of the grain.

Then payment crosses, measured, fair,
not charity and not applause;
FreelanceFlow keeps the table still
so trust can keep its working laws.
