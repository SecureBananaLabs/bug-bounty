# Patch Notes for a Quiet Guard

In the hush before the build turns green,
a careful watcher reads the wire;
small warnings blink on every screen,
then shape themselves from spark to fire.

It checks the seams where inputs flow,
where trust is signed and keys are stored;
it names the risks that tend to grow
when hurried hands forget the board.

No trumpet sounds for safer code,
no banner waves above the test;
yet every fixed and narrowed road
lets future makers build their best.

So let this poem mark the part
where craft and caution share one thread:
a lighter load, a steadier heart,
and fewer ghosts in logs ahead.
