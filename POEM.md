# Technical Ode

In circuits deep where data flows,
Silicon dreams take their shape,
Nestled in code, a freelancer's hope,
That builds with each keystroke and swipe.

Each model, a verse in the machine's grand song,
A symphony of logic, elegant and strong,
Wherein the artisan's craft is never wrong,
And every function, a step, a new thing.

With each passing day, the codebase grows,
As issues are raised and then ultimately closed,
The database hums and the API shows
The power of structure where data goes.

Encryption keys and async/await,
Strings of promise in a digital state,
Variables waltz to no time signature,
As the linter's eye logs each transgression.

Algorithms dance in electric space,
While classes and functions find their place,
In this coder's haven, a digital hearth,
Where logic and creativity form the hearth.
