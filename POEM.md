# Trust Ledger

The queue wakes softly, green checks in a row,  
Each promise wrapped in tests before it flies.  
A marketplace is more than bids that glow;  
It is the quiet proof behind replies.

An escrow hums beneath the checkout light,  
Holding the line where risk and hope divide.  
The worker ships, the client sleeps at night,  
And every state change leaves a trace beside.

So build the page where honest signals stay,  
Where logs remember what the screen forgot.  
Let payment, work, and trust move one clear way,  
Small verified steps instead of fragile thought.
