# The Builder's Code

*For SecureBananaLabs and all who hunt bugs in the night*

---

## I. The Call

In circuits deep where errors sleep,
A bounty hunter wakes.
With keyboard sword and coffee poured,
The heated chase awaits.

Each issue born from midnight scorn,
A puzzle to unfold—
The glint of gold in stories told
Of problems brave and bold.

---

## II. The Craft

No bug too small, no race too tall,
The hunter tests and tries.
With automated might and sleepless nights,
They code where silence lies.

A failing test, a doubtful rest,
Then green at last appears.
The PR sent, the time well spent,
Through doubt and trials and fears.

---

## III. The Reward

For those who build and refuse to yield,
Who automate the grind,
The bounty pays in more than praise—
Each merge a peace of mind.

So raise a toast to those who post
Their time and skill and art,
The hunters few who see it through
And code with open heart.

---

*Theme: The modern bounty hunter's journey from problem to solution.*  
*Form: Ballad meter (8.6.8.6) for rhythm of iteration.*
