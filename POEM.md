# The Merge Window

*For FreelanceFlow — a technical poem in three stanzas*

---

The proposal lands at midnight, timestamped, signed.
A stranger's scope, your estimate refined —
one hundred hours, your craft against their clock.
The escrow waits. The ledger holds the lock.

You build in layers: schema, route, and view,
each pull request a promise you pursue.
The CI runner passes, green to green,
and trust accumulates in what's unseen —

a comment thread, a diff, a merged commit.
Then money moves: the payout executes, the bit
is settled. Two strangers, code between them, reach
the terms that no handshake alone could teach.

---

*FreelanceFlow — where every job listing, proposal, and payment is a transaction in trust.*
