# Verified Handoff

The brief arrives as a narrow lane,
with budget, deadline, and the work made plain.
A job stops being a wish in flight
when scope is named and the terms sit tight.

The proposal answers with measured hands:
what will be built, what proof it demands.
Messages keep the edges clean,
no missing promise left between.

The API passes the order through,
Prisma records what each side knew.
Status, payment, review, and role
turn scattered effort into a whole.

The dashboard waits with quiet proof:
a test run, a diff, a finished truth.
The client checks what the notes explain;
the freelancer patches the last loose pane.

Then merge becomes a signed receipt,
not just code on a passing street.
FreelanceFlow makes the handoff clear:
work, evidence, payout, and trust appear.
