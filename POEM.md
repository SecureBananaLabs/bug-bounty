# Proof Window

Scope opens first, a pane of measured light,
where every promise learns its edge and name;
the brief is small enough to hold in sight,
the work begins before it asks for fame.

Messages gather like a steady trace,
each question pinned beside the answer found;
no hidden hallway, no uncertain place,
just context laid where trust can make its ground.

Validation taps the glass with patient care,
rejecting noise before it joins the stream;
the clean request is shaped enough to share,
the broken one returns with what it means.

Review arrives with evidence in hand,
not thunder, just a diff that can be read;
the runner marks what passed, what needs to stand,
and leaves a trail for anyone ahead.

Payout is quiet when the proof is clear,
a final pane lit gold against the dark;
the handoff closes, certain and sincere,
and future work begins from that small mark.
