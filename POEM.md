# Ledger at First Light

Between the brief and opened thread,
A client names the work ahead.
A builder reads the scope line through,
Then turns a promise into true.

The bids arrive with measured care,
With rates, receipts, and records there.
Trust is not a badge alone;
It grows from proof and work well shown.

When payments wait in guarded lanes,
The platform counts the risks and gains.
Each message, file, and milestone
Keeps shared intent from drifting lone.

If flags appear or disputes rise,
The panel asks for clearer eyes.
Fair hands review, resolve, restore,
Then send the marketplace once more.
