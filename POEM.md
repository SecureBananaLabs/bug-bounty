# The Route of Trust

A brief leaves the harbor with measured light,
its scope tied down like cargo in the rain;
the client names the shore, the builder sets the sight,
and small true tasks begin to cross the chain.

In quiet rooms the evidence is made:
a test that holds, a note that shows the shape,
a screen that proves the promise did not fade,
and turns a moving ticket into something seen.

Reviewers walk the bridge with careful hands,
not hunting noise, but weight the code can bear;
each comment maps the place where trust expands,
each green check opens cleaner working air.

Then merge becomes a gate, not just a mark,
where craft and payment meet without disguise;
the ledger keeps a lantern in the dark,
so labor has a path, and proof a price.

When reputation rises from the flow,
it is not luck, nor loudness, nor a claim;
it is the habit of delivered work we know,
and every honest handoff signs its name.
