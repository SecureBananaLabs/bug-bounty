# POEM.md — "The Guardian's Vigil"

## The Firewall's Dream

Through ports of endless, shifting light,
Where packets dance in coded flight,
A sentinel of logic stands,
With silicon and silent hands.
It watches, learns, and understands
The patterns of the darkest night.

## The Key and the Gate

The gate asks not for blood or name,
But for the key of trusted claim.
Each handshake signed, each byte aware,
The protocol beyond compare—
A digital and patient care
That tames the chaos and the flame.

## The Dawn of Trust

So let the clever logic learn,
And let the careful system turn.
The battles fought in silent room,
Where nothing bleeds but something blooms—
A safer world inside the gloom,
Where human hopes and circuits yearn.
