# The Silent Merge

*A poem about the quiet moments in code*

---

At 3 AM the terminal glows,
a cursor blinks where no one goes,
the test suite hums its steady song—
green, green, green—the build is strong.

I wrote the function, wrote the guard,
the edge case others disregard,
a null check here, a timeout there,
a whispered prayer into the air.

The diff is clean, the lint is passed,
no warnings left, no shadows cast,
I push the branch and close the tab,
a quiet commit—*fix: grab*.

Somewhere in Tokyo, a dev
reads my PR before their coffee,
drops a comment: "LGTM 👍"
and merges me into the stream.

No ceremony, no applause,
just bytes that shift without a pause,
the repo grows by forty lines,
and somewhere, something better shines.

I never meet the ones who read
the docs I wrote at 3 AM,
but every `npm install` that works
is proof that someone gave a damn.

---

*For every contributor who ships in silence.*
