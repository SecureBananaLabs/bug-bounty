# POEM.md — Ode to the Bug Hunter

## The Quiet Watch

Beneath the surface, pixels stream,
A universe of code and dream.
One misplaced semicolon's gleam,
Becomes the crack in the regime.

The hunter sits with steady hand,
Across the digital command.
A stack trace whispers through the land,
A story no one understands.

---

## The Hunt

Through endless loops and memory's maze,
Through tangled imports, nested phrase,
The hunter walks the shadowed ways,
And counts the cost in silent days.

A race condition, subtle, small,
A null that shouldn't fall at all.
An edge case waiting for the call,
To bring the mighty system's thrall.

---

## The Find

And then, a flicker in the deep,
A bug that's been awhile asleep.
A secret that the code would keep,
Before the walls begin to leap.

The patch is written, clean and tight,
The diff is small, the logic right.
A single PR against the night,
To bring the broken back to light.

---

## The Fix

No trophy stands upon the shelf,
No banner raised for private self.
But in the codebase's quiet health,
The hunter finds their truest wealth.

For every fix, the system grows,
A stronger tree from what we chose.
And deep inside, each hunter knows:
This peace is earned by facing woes.

---

*Written for the bug-bounty project — a poem about the quiet guardians of code who find beauty in the hunt for bugs and the craft of fixing them.*
*Theme chosen: The unsung heroism of security researchers and bug bounty hunters.*
*Form chosen: Four stanzas of iambic tetrameter, echoing the rhythm of code.*
