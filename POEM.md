# Escrow Clockwork

At sunrise, scope is wound by hand,
each promise cut to measured teeth;
the client names the shape of done,
the maker sets the spring beneath.

A message ticks from rail to rail,
with proof attached and context clear;
small gears of trust begin to turn,
and blocked assumptions disappear.

The review room keeps a steady light,
not bright enough to hide a flaw;
it checks the craft, records the path,
and leaves a trace for those who saw.

When milestones click into their marks,
the payout gate unlocks with care;
reputation moves another notch,
earned in public, settled fair.

So FreelanceFlow should run like this:
brief, build, inspect, release, repeat;
a quiet clock for honest work,
where every hand and hour meet.
