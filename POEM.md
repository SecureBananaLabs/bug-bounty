# The Narrow Bridge

An issue opens like a signal in the dark,
a small red pulse beside a line of work.
We do not chase the whole horizon.
We draw the boundary, name the fault,
and carry one clean fix across.

Evidence waits in the quiet after build,
where logs are less opinion than weather.
A test turns green, a diff stays small,
and every claim worth making
keeps its proof within reach.

Review is a narrow bridge,
not a stage for noise or speed.
Step by step, the patch walks over:
scope, reason, result, receipt,
each plank holding because it was checked.

Then payout is only the final light,
not the work itself, not the measure of care.
The better craft is what survives the wait:
a trail another maintainer can follow,
and trust that does not need a speech.
