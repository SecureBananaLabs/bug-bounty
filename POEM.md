# Bounty Hunter's Evening

The merge window closes, the linter sleeps.  
A stray bug stirs in the dependency deeps.  
One unclosed span, one unchecked cast —  
The ghost in the pipeline is never the last.

The bounty board flickers, a neon sea.  
"Repro steps attached. Will pay BTC."  
He traces the stack through the dusk of the log,  
a phantom in middleware, a curl in the fog.

The commit lands clean, the reviewer nods.  
Somewhere a server exhales its gods.  
He closes the laptop, the screen goes dark.  
Another crack fixed in the monolith's ark.

And the bug that wakes in the morning light  
will seed itself in some new commit tonight.  
The hunt doesn't end — it just changes face.  
There's always a hole in the stateful grace.
