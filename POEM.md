# The Pulse of the Flow

### I. The Blueprint (Prisma)
In the silence of the server, where the databases sleep,  
Prisma stands as keeper of the secrets that we keep.  
A `User` with a custom role, a `cuid` for their name,  
A `CLIENT` or a `FREELANCER` who joins the modern game.  
The `Job` is born in draft-state, with a category defined,  
As lines of strict TypeScript logic keep our schemas aligned.  

### II. The Journey (Next.js & Express)
From `apps/web` the Next.js routes begin to map the space,  
An interactive client view, a polished interface.  
The proposals stream like stardust through the Express.js gateway,  
Validation schemas (`Zod`) checking data on the highway.  
A `Message` is dispatched and read, the UI comes alive,  
As freelancer and client meet, and workflows start to thrive.  

### III. The Exchange (Stripe)
Behind the scenes the payments wait to trigger the release,  
A Stripe reference is registered, all friction starts to cease.  
The budget max, the budget min, the escrow locked in code,  
Secure transaction channels on the digital high road.  
A database mutation runs, the status turns complete,  
And reviews with five-star ratings make the long endeavor sweet.  

### IV. The Flow (Aspirations)
We are the pulse, the digital thread, the architects of flow,  
Where dreams of independent minds in modern systems grow.  
Through packages and applications, nested side-by-side,  
With FreelanceFlow we code the future, standing tall with pride.  
So let the server listen, and the web components play,  
For every byte we write tonight empowers someone's day.
