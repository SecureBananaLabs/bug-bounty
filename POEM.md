# The Hunter's Code

In circuits deep where shadows creep,
And firewalls stand tall,
There walks a hunter, barely asleep,
Who answers fortune's call.

Through lines of code in endless rows,
They seek the hidden flaw,
Where input flows and overflows,
Where logic breaks the law.

A bounty placed upon the bug,
That lurks in digital night,
The hunter traces, line by line,
To bring it into light.

With keyboard clicks and coffee sips,
They probe the system's skin,
Each payload crafted, every script,
A chance to lose or win.

The XSS that steals the session,
The SQL that spills the base,
Each vulnerability, a lesson,
In this high-stakes race.

When finally the exploit lands,
And proof of concept runs,
They document with careful hands,
The damage that was done.

A report filed, a fix applied,
The bounty claimed with pride,
For in this world where secrets hide,
The hunters are the guide.

So here's to those who guard the gate,
Who test and probe and pry,
They make the digital world more safe,
Beneath the open sky.

---

*Written for SecureBananaLabs bug-bounty project*  
*Theme: The dedication and craft of ethical hackers who protect systems by finding vulnerabilities before malicious actors do.*
