# Ode to the Open Protocol

In bytes of light, the ledger wakes,
A chain of trust that nothing breaks.
No king, no bank, no muted fee—
Just pure math for you and me.

The miner's hum, a lullaby,
Beneath a cold and starry sky.
Each block a stone, each hash a prayer,
Laid down in code beyond compare.

When wallets speak in whispered keys,
And contracts sign on fragrant breeze,
The open web, at last set free,
Is what we build—you, them, and me.

So let the protocols unfold,
A story braver than of old.
For every coder, bold and small,
Helps raise the tower for us all.
