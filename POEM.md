# The Flow of Code and Commerce

## A Poem for FreelanceFlow

In digital halls where seekers roam,
Through lines of code and bits of chrome,
A marketplace of skill and dream,
Where freelancers and clients stream.

The morning starts with coffee's steam,
A proposal crafted, hopes to gleam,
The web app hums on Next.js bright,
While Express routes guide through day and night.

Prisma schemas hold the truth,
Of users, jobs, and payment's sooth,
The monorepo stands so tall,
TypeScript types protect us all.

From landing page to dashboard deep,
Where messages and notifs sleep,
The frontend dances, React aglow,
The backend answers, steady, slow.

A client posts, a job takes form,
The marketplace begins to warm,
With skills and categories aligned,
Great work and trust are what we find.

So here's to flows of freelance free,
To open source and community,
May every bug be found and tamed,
And every pull request be named.

---

*Creative decision: I chose a traditional AABB rhyme scheme with iambic meter to create a playful, accessible tone that mirrors the "friendly" and "good first issue" labels on this bounty. The poem weaves in specific technical references (Next.js, Express, Prisma, TypeScript, monorepo) to celebrate the project's architecture while keeping the spirit welcoming for new contributors.*
