# The Bridge of Code

Between the human mind and silicon dream,
A bridge of logic stretches taut and lean,
Where every function is a stepping stone,
And every variable a seed that's sown.

We write in tongues of Python, TypeScript,
And watch our thoughts take shape in swift, clean light,
From waterfall to agile stream they flow,
Through pull requests where critiques grow.

The debugger's pause, the failing test,
The sudden insight born from restless quest,
Through endless loops and recursive descent,
We chase the bug until its will is spent.

So here we stand, at edge of vast unknown,
With open source as seed that we have sown,
Each contribution — merge, commit, or star —
A distant node, a bridge, a guiding star.
