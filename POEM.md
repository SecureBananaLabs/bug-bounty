# The Banana's Shield

A bug bounty blooms in the dead of the night,  
Where hackers and hunters prepare for the fight.  
With payloads compiled and terminals green,  
They search for the secrets left out and unseen.  

SecureBanana stands tall on the net,  
A fortress of nodes they have carefully set.  
With middlewares scanning each token and key,  
To lock down the borders for you and for me.  

So bring on the fuzzers and proxy your flows,  
For every new finding is one that we chose.  
We learn from the chaos, we patch up the code,  
And safely continue along the sweet road.
