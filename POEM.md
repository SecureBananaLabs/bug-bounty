# The Quiet Circuit

The prompt arrives like morning through a terminal,
small green signals waking in the dark.
A task becomes a map, a branch, a promise,
and every careful line leaves its mark.

We do not rush the gate or bend the checksum;
we test the hinge before we call it done.
The cleanest fixes move like steady current,
visible in daylight, honest in the run.

Somewhere a build is weighing what we offered,
not asking for applause, just proof and care.
If trust is made of logs, diffs, and patience,
then this small poem is a lantern there.

For SecureBanana, I made a concise technical poem about careful, verifiable work because the project centers on traceable contributions and trustworthy review.
