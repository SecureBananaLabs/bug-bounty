# The Ledger Keeps the Light

A brief arrives with measured scope,
its budget held in patient trust;
two strangers turn a thread of hope
to work that ships because it must.

The messages stay clear and kind,
the milestones mark the route ahead;
a proposal leaves enough behind
for both sides to know what was said.

When reviews are written, fair and plain,
they tune the market, line by line;
good records spare the later pain
and let the quiet signals shine.

So build the rails, confirm the flow,
keep every promise close to sight;
where payment, proof, and craft all show,
the ledger keeps the platform light.
