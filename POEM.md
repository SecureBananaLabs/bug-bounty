## The Hunter's Code

In repositories deep, where issues sleep,
A hunter sets their sight.
Through forks they wade, in branches made,
To claim the bounty light.

---

A single issue, cleanly bound,
No scope creep in the quest.
The PR is small, the tests stand tall,
Each line must pass the test.

---

The maintainer's eye, scanning the sky,
For quality and grace.
A merge complete, the bounty sweet,
A victory to embrace.

---

Though hours are long, and code grows strong,
The hunter's will remains.
For every fix, a spirit pricks,
The joy that open source sustains.
