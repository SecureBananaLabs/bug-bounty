# The Flow of Work

## I. The Connection

In circuits of code where the packets align,  
A client with vision, a freelancer's design.  
No borders, no offices, no clock on the wall,  
Just Prisma and Next.js connecting us all.  
From OAuth to JWT, the auth flows like streams,  
While WebSockets whisper in async dreams.  

## II. The Marketplace

A job post appears in the digital square,  
Proposals fly fast through the crisp morning air.  
Zod validates shapes, middleware stands guard,  
As payments await Stripe's cryptographic card.  
Reviews pile up like leaves in the fall,  
Ratings and stars — reputation for all.  

## III. The Promise

No middleman takes what the worker has earned,  
In FreelanceFlow's ledger, trust is returned.  
From monorepo roots to the frontend's bright view,  
We build something fair, something open, something new.  
So commit your best work, let the CI run green,  
In this flow of freelance, the future is seen.
