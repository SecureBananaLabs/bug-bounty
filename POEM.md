# The Flow of Code and Coin

## A Poem for FreelanceFlow

In digital halls where seekers roam,
Through lines of code and pixels' home,
A marketplace of skill and dream,
Where talent flows like a steady stream.

The client posts upon the wire,
A job, a need, a burning fire,
And freelancers from far and wide,
Bring craft and art to stand beside.

From schema.prisma's structured grace,
To API's embracing space,
The layers speak in RESTful tongue,
Where services and routes are strung.

No more the gig of cold despair,
But dashboards built with craft and care,
With messages that bridge the night,
And payments that arrive on sight.

So here's to flows that freely run,
To jobs well done and battles won,
To every dev who finds their way,
Through FreelanceFlow, come what may.

---

*Written for the FreelanceFlow monorepo — a tribute to the architecture that connects builders with dreamers, and the modern stack that makes it all possible.*

---

**Creative Decision:** I chose a traditional AABB rhyme scheme with iambic meter to create a deliberate contrast with the modern tech stack described in the README. The poem weaves together specific technical elements (Prisma, REST API, dashboards, payments) with the human experience of freelancing, reflecting how FreelanceFlow bridges code and community. The tone is hopeful and celebratory — mirroring the project's goal of making freelance work more flowing and secure.
