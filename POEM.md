# The Digital Banana Grove

## A Poem for SecureBananaLabs

In circuits deep where data flows,
A banana stands, a sentinel bold,
Not yellow fruit that monkeys know,
But crypto-keys in digital gold.

---

The lab hums soft with server song,
Of hashes, blocks, and encrypted streams,
Where vulnerabilities don't belong,
And security is more than dreams.

Each line of code, a careful seed,
Planted in the open source soil,
Growing tools that the world needs,
Born from developer toil.

---

We are the monkeys of the new age,
Swinging through branches of the cloud,
Building walls against the rage,
Of hackers lurking in the crowd.

Our bananas guard the gate,
With peels of fire and cryptographic might,
Ensuring data stays innate,
Protected through the darkest night.

---

So here's to SecureBananaLabs,
Where creativity meets the code,
Where innovation freely flows,
And security is the road.

May our pixels shine bright,
And our poems tell the tale,
Of a future built right,
Where security will prevail.

---

*Written for SecureBananaLabs with appreciation for their innovative approach to cybersecurity and open-source bounty programs.*
