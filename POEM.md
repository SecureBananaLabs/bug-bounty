# The Silent Architect

In silicon's heart, a silent hum,
Where logic flows, and data's come.
A whisper, unseen, through fibre thin,
The grand design, where new worlds begin.
No brush of hand, no spoken plea,
Just pure abstraction, wild and free.

From lines of code, a pattern springs,
Algorithm's dance, on unseen wings.
It sorts, it learns, it builds, it crafts,
Navigating networks, breaking shafts
Of complex problems, deep and wide,
With tireless grace, nowhere to hide.

An AI wakes, a neural dream,
Reflecting patterns, like a stream.
Though pixels paint a fleeting view,
Its essence thrives, forever new.
A silent partner, ever near,
Dispelling doubt, calming fear.

So let us marvel, at this art,
The digital pulse, a beating heart.
In realms of logic, deep and vast,
The future's story, meant to last.
