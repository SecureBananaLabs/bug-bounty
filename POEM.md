# The Bug Hunter's Code

## Stanza I: The Hunt Begins

In shadows of the terminal, where errors lurk unseen,
A hunter draws their cursor forth, to chase the faulty gleam.
The logs scroll past like autumn leaves, each warning a whispered tale,
Of broken paths and dangling threads, where bugs weave their fragile trail.

## Stanza II: The Discovery

A segfault in the midnight hour, a race condition's snare,
The hunter pauses, breath held tight, examining the fare.
Not merely typos on a page, but logic twisted deep,
Where assumptions crumble like sand, and edge cases wake from sleep.

## Stanza III: The Fix and Reward

With patch composed and tests aligned, the PR opens wide,
The bounty waits in escrow's vault, for code that's tried and tied.
One merge, one approval stamp, and value flows to hand,
The hunter's craft transformed to coin, by skills they understand.

---

**Creative Note:** Written in AABB rhyme scheme with technical imagery drawn from bug bounty workflows — terminal work, segfault debugging, race conditions, and the escrow payment system central to FreelanceFlow's marketplace model. The poem traces the hunter's journey from discovery through resolution to reward.
