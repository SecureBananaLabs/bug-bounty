# The Handshake Ledger

A client pins a need to the morning board,
a freelancer answers with scope kept clean,
between them waits a measured chord,
not trust by promise, but trust made seen.

JWTs keep the doorways named,
Zod checks each field before it can speak,
messages carry the work unclaimed
until both sides know what proof they seek.

An invoice sleeps behind the gate,
Stripe marks the sum in patient light,
the dashboard counts each changed state,
and turns vague progress into sight.

Reviews arrive like careful glass,
clear enough to show the line:
what was shipped, what still must pass,
what work is yours, what work is mine.

So FreelanceFlow holds each handoff still,
from posted brief to paid release,
a ledger built from craft and will,
where finished work can leave in peace.
