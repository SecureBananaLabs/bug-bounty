# The Compiler's Lullaby

In silicon dreams where logic flows,
Through gates of AND and OR it goes,
Each semicolon, bracket, brace—
A universe in typed-out space.

We debug the night till morning breaks,
Chase phantom bugs for sanity's stakes,
Yet in the glow of monitors bright,
We find a strange and pure delight.

The functions call, the loops repeat,
Like heartbeats steady, rhythms sweet,
From chaos born, through order grown,
Each line of code a seed we've sown.

So here's to those who build and dream,
Who craft the logic, scheme by scheme,
For in this craft of ones and zeros,
We find our art, we are the heroes.
