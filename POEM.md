# Latency of a Promise

In the marketplace before sunrise,
profiles hum like lanterns in a queue;
each bid is a small bright packet
looking for the work it was meant to do.

A client writes a need in plain text,
a freelancer answers with craft and time;
between them, routes keep their quiet balance,
turning trust into something that can climb.

The dashboard counts what the heart cannot:
messages, milestones, invoices, names;
yet under every measured transaction
is a human hope with no two frames the same.

So let the servers answer cleanly,
let every request return with grace;
the fastest path is not just speed,
but finding the right person in the right place.
