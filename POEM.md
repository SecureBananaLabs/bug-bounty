# Calibration Bench for FreelanceFlow

On the calibration bench, the brief arrives as weight,
not rumor, not weather, not a guess in flight.
The client sets scope where the clean marks wait,
and FreelanceFlow holds the measure to the light.

A proposal clicks into a numbered tray,
hours, terms, and trust aligned in rows.
Escrow does not sing; it simply stays
until the work can show what the promise chose.

The branch is quiet while the patch is made,
each test a level, each diff a gauge.
Evidence settles where doubt once strayed,
clear enough for review to turn the page.

Comments become filings, exact and thin,
removing the burr from an uncertain line.
The runner turns green, and the handoff begins
with proof more durable than a confident dream.

When payout closes, nothing needs to shout.
The record remains with its checksum bright:
scope weighed in, delivery weighed out,
and trust recalibrated for the next good write.
