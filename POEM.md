# The Safeguard of the Golden Peel

In the quiet of the codebase, where the threads and queries spin,  
A freelance portal rises up to let the builders in.  
But rogue exploits and shadow threats would seek to claim the prize,  
So we wrap the system’s architecture in a shield of yellow guise.  

Through Prisma’s strict schema types and Postgres’ solid store,  
No injection-heavy payload shall pass the gated door.  
With Next.js on the client side and Express to serve the call,  
We validate the Stripe intents and guard the borders of it all.  

From client up to freelancer, a trusted bond is made,  
While automated rules stand watch to ensure the fee is paid.  
No malicious extensions here, no e-skimming to fear,  
For the yellow peel of safety keeps the marketplace crystal clear.  

So let the benchmarks run their course and latencies descend,  
We stand as sovereign guardians from beginning unto end.  
In the grand republic of the web, where coding bounties shine,  
SecureBanana stands secure, robust in every line.
