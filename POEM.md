# The Trust Turnstile

At the gate of a brief, a clear scope clicks through,
One ticket, one promise, one path to pursue.
The client sets context, the builder sets pace,
And FreelanceFlow keeps every handoff in place.

A proposal arrives with its estimate tied
To milestones that neither side needs to hide.
The messages carry the why and the when,
So intent can be found and inspected again.

The dashboard stays patient while evidence grows:
Commits, review notes, and the work as it shows.
No handshake is vague when the record is near,
And progress can answer before doubt can appear.

When billing lights up, it is not just a fee;
It marks that the task and the proof both agree.
The payout moves forward through verifiable ground,
With each status change leaving a trace to be found.

Then reviews write the closing line into the frame:
Trust is a system, not luck with a name.
From first posted need to the final release,
FreelanceFlow turns scoped work into peace.
