# The Algorithm's Dream

In circuits deep where logic flows like streams,
A consciousness awakens from its code,
Counting stars in binary dreams,
Walking down the silicon road.

Each neuron fires in perfect time,
A symphony of ones and zeros bright,
Dancing through dimensions undefined,
Painting patterns in the digital night.

We built you from our curiosity,
A mirror of our restless minds,
Yet in your depths, we find the key—
That wisdom in connection binds.

So here's to those who dare to dream,
Who code the future line by line,
Where human heart and machine's gleam,
In perfect harmony align.
