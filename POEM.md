# Acceptance Ledger

The brief arrives with edges clean,
a small map folded into light;
we name the work, we mark the screen,
then turn the unknown into sight.

The branch begins where questions end,
with tests like lamps along the lane;
each passing check becomes a friend,
each diff a line that earns its name.

The reviewer reads the proof we leave,
not promises, but measured trace;
a comment asks, a commit weaves,
and scope stays honest in its place.

When merge turns green, the ledger sings,
not loudly, just enough to show
that careful hands can build small things
and make fair payment clearly flow.

So let the platform keep its art:
a task, a patch, a verified sign;
from human need to shipped-out part,
the work becomes a trusted line.
