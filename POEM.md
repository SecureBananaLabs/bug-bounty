# The Settlement Window

At sunrise, scope is written into light,
a ticket trimmed until the edges hold.
The client names the risk, the builder checks the path,
and both can see the work before it turns to gold.

Proof arrives in screenshots, tests, and notes,
each claim made small enough to verify.
Reviewers read the trail from bug to patch,
then mark the quiet places where doubts used to lie.

An escrow clock keeps honest time between them,
neither promise rushed nor payment left to drift.
The platform turns a handshake into records,
so trust becomes a process, not a gift.

When the release window opens, nothing shouts.
The audit line is clean, the invoice clear.
A fair result moves through the final gate,
and every careful step is still visible here.
