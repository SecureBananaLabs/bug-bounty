# The Debugger's Creed

*For the hunters of hidden flaws and silent errors*

---

**I. The Hunt Begins**

At midnight when the build turns red,
A shadow stirs in lines of thread.
The stack unwinds, the trace reveals
A ghost that in the register steals.
With eyes that parse through thousand lines,
I track the fault between the signs.

**II. The Chase**

Through loops that spiral without end,
On leaky promises we depend.
The null ref hides in wrapped disguise,
A race condition's clever lies.
Breakpoint by breakpoint, step by step,
I trace the logic's tangled web.

**III. The Victory**

And there 鈥?one line, one missing guard,
The flaw that made the system hard.
A patch, a test, a green deploy,
The bug that lurked is bug no more.
For every crash and every stall,
The debugger answers duty's call.

**IV. The Philosophy**

A bounty hunter's honest creed:
Make code more stable, plant the seed
Of defensive code and thoughtful tests,
So future hackers get more rest.
For every flaw we squash today
Keeps one more breach of trust at bay.
