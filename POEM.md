# The Verification Room

A brief arrives with quiet edges,
its scope pinned flat beneath the light;
we name the risks, we trim the pledges,
and keep the promised boundary tight.

A message thread becomes a ledger,
each question dated, plain, and clear;
no hidden hand behind the measure,
no missing proof for work held dear.

The tests wake up like signal lanterns,
green when the contract still holds true;
red when a path forgets its pattern,
bright enough to guide the view.

Reviewers walk the change in order,
from diff to demo, line to claim;
they mark the seam where trust grows broader,
and leave a record with a name.

Then merge and payout share one doorway,
not rushed, not vague, not left to chance;
the room grows still, the work moves forward,
and evidence completes the dance.
