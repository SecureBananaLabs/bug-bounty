# The Ledger Keeps Its Lantern

In the market where bright builders gather,
briefs arrive like sparks at dawn;
a client names the shape of needed work,
and careful hands make trust move on.

The gateway hums behind the counter,
counting bids, reviews, and time;
every token asks for proof before passage,
every promise earns its line.

A message crosses from one maker
to one buyer, clear and signed;
not a shadow, not a shortcut,
just the trail that leaves fraud behind.

So let the tests be small, but steady;
let the routes reject the wrong;
secure flows turn scattered labor
into something built to last long.

When the final pull request settles,
and the green checks softly shine,
the ledger keeps its lantern burning:
work, verified, becomes design.
