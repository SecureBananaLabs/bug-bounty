# The Bounty Hunter's Code

In midnight glow, the screen burns bright,
A hunter stalks the digital night.
Through nested loops and tangled threads,
They seek the flaws that cause the dreads.

A missing check, an off-by-one,
A race condition, never done.
The stack trace whispers, line by line,
Where logic fails and shadows twine.

With coffee cold and eyes aflame,
They fork the repo, stake their claim.
The fix is small, the impact grand,
One merge request could change the land.

The reviewer comes with eagle eyes,
And checks the diff through sharpened guise.
The tests run green, the builds pass clean,
The cleanest code that's ever been.

When morning breaks and stars align,
The bounty bot says "Registered claim complete."
One thousand dollars transferred through the wire,
The hunter rests, their task entire.

So here's to those who hunt the bugs,
Who squash the errors, kill the slugs.
In open source, we find our worth,
One pull request per day on Earth.
