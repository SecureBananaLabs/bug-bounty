# Zero Trust Orchard

In the orchard where the build lights glow,
each secret waits behind a gate;
no branch is trusted just for shade,
no token walks without its weight.

The pipeline hums in measured lines,
hashes bloom where doubts once stood;
a failing check becomes a flare,
and silence learns to do some good.

We peel the risk from every path,
one small review, one tightened key;
what ships at dawn should stand at night,
plain in its proof, and clean to see.

So guard the fruit, but keep it kind:
let warnings speak before they burn;
the safest systems are not loud,
they teach the next deploy to learn.
