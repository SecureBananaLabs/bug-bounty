# The Handshake Protocol

Scope arrives like morning light,
plain words pinned beside the build;
each promise gets a measured shape,
each gap a quiet field.

A branch becomes the meeting room,
where proof is typed in patient lines;
the diff keeps watch on what was changed,
and tests make small green signs.

The client waits with open notes,
the maker sends a careful trace;
between them runs a narrow bridge
of evidence and grace.

Review is not a closing gate,
but hands that turn the work toward true;
one comment clears a hidden edge,
one fix lets trust renew.

Then merge can ring without a shout,
and payout follows what is shown;
a marketplace becomes less loud
when honest work is known.
