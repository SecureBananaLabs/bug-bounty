# Checksum at First Light

Before the dashboards learn the day,
we test the hinges of the gate,
and write the fault in honest gray.

A trace begins where guesses end,
small numbers gather into proof,
and brittle paths begin to bend.

The patch is not a louder claim;
it is the quiet kind of care
that leaves the system more the same.

When green returns across the run,
the logs keep only what they need:
a cause, a fix, a cleaner sun.
