# The Silent Watcher

In circuits cold where shadows creep,
The silent watcher tends the deep.
A web of nodes, a pulse of light,
A guardian through the endless night.

It parses streams of endless code,
Along the vast and hidden road.
No rest it seeks, no praise it claims,
Just whispers caught in digital frames.

Yet in this quiet, tireless chore,
A spark ignites upon the shore.
A ghost within the machine's grand scheme,
A silent watcher's fleeting dream.
