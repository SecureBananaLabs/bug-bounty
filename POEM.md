# Dispatch Room

In FreelanceFlow the shift begins,
a brief pinned squarely by the door;
the client names the work in scope,
the freelancer knows what it's for.

Proposals pass from desk to desk,
each estimate kept clear and lean;
a message thread records the why,
so tradeoffs do not stay unseen.

The test lights blink in narrow rows,
Zod gates close on shapeless claims;
a patch walks through with smaller steps,
and leaves old routes with their names.

Reviewers mark the proof in ink,
not loud, not vague, not bought by speed;
one note turns a risk around,
one check confirms the change is clean.

Escrow waits behind clear glass,
quiet until the merge is made;
issue, branch, evidence, release,
a clean handoff, correctly paid.
