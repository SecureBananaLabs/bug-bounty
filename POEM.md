# The Handoff Ledger

The brief arrives with edges sharp and clear,
A promise shaped from scope, constraint, and need;
FreelanceFlow keeps the working record near,
Where trust can move at engineering speed.

A proposal learns the weight of every line,
The bid, the dates, the skill set, and the proof;
Each field becomes a quiet checkout sign,
Each test a beam beneath the marketplace roof.

The messages hold context in their frame,
No handshake lost outside the reviewable trail;
A milestone changes state and keeps its name,
So neither side must bargain through the veil.

The payment waits until the work is shown,
Then leaves a marker both accounts can read;
Good software makes the uncertain parts known,
And pays the people who complete the deed.

So let the dashboard keep its steady light,
From posted job to merge, from bill to view;
The cleanest handoff is not loud or bright,
It simply proves what honest builders do.
