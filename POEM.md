# The Proof Between Hands

Scope is the first clean line we draw,  
not a promise shouted into noise,  
but a boundary bright enough to hold  
the work, the risk, the choice.

Evidence arrives in patient rows:  
a failing step, a patched reply,  
a diff that turns a hidden fault  
into something reviewers can try.

Review keeps the doorway lit,  
with patient eyes and records near;  
each note refines the shape of trust  
until the useful parts appear.

The merge is not a finish bell;  
it is a receipt the team can read,  
where trust moves through the open record  
from stated claim to tested deed.

Then payout follows, plain and fair,  
not luck, not volume, not display,  
but scoped work carried through review  
and honored in the light of day.
