# Ledger of the Quiet Hunt

We do not break the gate to prove we can—
we map the hinge, the latch, the unseen seam,
and leave a note where builders understand:
here warmth leaked out; here trust was thin as steam.

Each issue is a lantern hung on wire,
a bounty tagged in green and numbered gold,
inviting hands that read before they fire,
and patch what strangers almost left untold.

So when the merge light turns from red to done,
remember: safety is a shared refrain—
not one loud crash, but many small acts won,
until the whole repository holds the rain.
