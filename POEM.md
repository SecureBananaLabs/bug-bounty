# Clear Ledger

The brief arrives with edges drawn,
a title, scope, and due review;
the work begins where guesses stop,
and every claim must leave a clue.

A proof is more than passing light,
more than a line that says it ran;
it is the trail from need to patch,
from open risk to measured plan.

The reviewer reads the quiet parts,
the branch, the test, the changed refrain;
if trust is built from small receipts,
each checked result reduces strain.

Then merge becomes a signed accord,
not thunder, just the record clear;
the maker, client, code, and pay
stand close enough for all to hear.

So FreelanceFlow keeps honest time:
scope before speed, proof before pride;
fair payout follows finished work,
with every handoff shown outside.
