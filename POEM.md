# The Loom of Proof

At dawn the scopes are measured out,
thin threads of work across the board;
a promise waits without a shout,
its edges named, its risks restored.

The test light passes through each strand,
not hunting praise, but finding grain;
what breaks is held in steady hand,
then woven back through proof again.

A reviewer marks the crossing line,
where claim and evidence agree;
the patch becomes a smaller sign
that trust can move deliberately.

When payout clears the quiet gate,
no trumpet needs to shake the room;
the cleanest work can celebrate
by leaving less for doubt to bloom.
