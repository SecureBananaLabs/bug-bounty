# The Quiet Escrow Room

Between the brief and keyboard light,
a scope card waits with edges square;
we weigh the ask, we name the flight,
then leave no hidden work in air.

Evidence folds in ordered rows:
a test, a note, a failing trace;
the smallest proof that clearly shows
why trust can keep a human face.

Reviewers pass a careful hand
across the diff's deliberate grain;
they do not need to understand
a flourish that explains no gain.

When green checks settle into view,
the merge becomes a simple door;
the invoice follows what is true,
not louder claims, not wanting more.

So payout leaves the escrow room
with quiet rails and counted time;
good work does not require bloom,
just honest scope and finished rhyme.
