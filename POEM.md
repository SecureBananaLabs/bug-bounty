# Receipt Window

In the market's quiet console,
a brief becomes a measured light;
scope is pinned like northern wire,
small enough to steer through night.

The client marks the acceptance line,
the builder folds each test in place;
between the diff and dashboard pulse,
trust learns to keep a readable face.

Escrow hums below the counter,
not a promise, not a plea;
just a clock that waits for evidence,
then lets the finished value free.

Reviewers walk the checksum path,
one green signal, then one more;
every claim must carry proof
before it reaches payout's door.

So let this platform trade in clarity:
brief, build, review, release;
where honest work can leave a trail,
and every receipt can sign for peace.
