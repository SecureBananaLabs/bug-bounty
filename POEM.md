## The Freelancer's Lament: A Poem of Code and Craft**

In the realm where logic meets the lamp,
Where semicolons dance and variables ramp,
The dreams of ones and zeros are born—
In the silent hum of servers, each morn.

The coder's fingers fly across the key,
Crafting worlds where thoughts and functions be,
Each class and function, a careful design,
Each query, a thought that entwine, entwine,
The digital realm with a vision so keen,
That in this modern age, the unseen
Muse takes form in circuits, not in flesh,
Yet lives and breathes in every codelet's soul,
As they build with precision, their heart and mind whole.
