# The Ledger of Work

A brief arrives like packet-light,
small scope wrapped tight in named intent;
a builder reads the edge of night,
and turns a need to blueprint sent.

The branch remembers every hand,
each test a lamp along the rail;
messages cross the market span,
while quiet logs preserve the trail.

No promise clears by charm or smoke,
only proof survives review;
the patch is weighed, the contract spoke,
and trust is paid when work is true.

So flow, FreelanceFlow, from ask to done,
through proposal, merge, and release;
let earned coins follow useful code,
and let clean handoffs purchase peace.
