# The Sentinel Build

In the quiet branch before the merge,
small tests blink green against the night.
A benchmark hums, a warning stirs,
and every diff asks to be right.

The scanner walks through public paths,
counts every edge where bugs can hide.
It leaves no key, it breaks no lock,
just maps the risk and steps aside.

Then humans read what bots have found,
turn signal into cleaner code.
A safer build, a calmer deploy,
a lighter pager on the road.

So ship the shield, but keep it kind:
no trick, no breach, no borrowed flame.
Good security earns its trust
by leaving better than it came.
