# Signal Room

The brief arrives with a measured light,
not thunder, not a banner in the sky,
just scope pinned cleanly to the board
and names for what must change.

A test lamp warms beside the diff,
small proof beside a careful hand;
the room grows quiet enough to hear
which promise the patch has made.

Reviewers tune the needles slowly,
risk, result, and reason aligned;
each question turns the work in place
until its edges hold.

Then payout waits beyond the merge,
not a prize flung loose in noise,
but a receipt for trust made visible
and labor carried all the way home.
