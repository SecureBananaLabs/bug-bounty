# The Merge Window

In the queue where tickets gather,
small green checks begin to glow;
a branch is just a question
until the tests can answer no.

The panel keeps its quiet ledger,
routes and roles and payments named;
every change leaves proof behind it,
every promise must be framed.

Review is not a ribbon,
not a flourish after code;
it is the lock on moving money,
and the light across the road.

So ship the smallest honest patch,
leave the trail clean, short, and clear;
when the merge window opens,
the work should still make sense from here.
