# The Audit Trail

At first the ticket lands in silence,
a narrow path through open code;
requirements draw their measured borders,
and questions mark the safest road.

The branch begins with small intentions,
a careful change, a tested seam;
each commit leaves a quiet footprint
across the landscape of the scheme.

Review arrives like evening lanterns,
casting light on hidden bends;
a comment turns a lock to handle,
a revision smooths what logic mends.

The pipeline keeps its steady vigil,
watching every step and sign;
failures speak in plain directions,
success leaves a cleaner line.

No anthem greets a passing build,
no crowd attends a merged request;
the work is known by what remains:
fewer faults and stronger tests.

And when the ledger marks completion,
reward follows the proven way;
for trust is not a sudden promise,
but evidence collected day by day.
