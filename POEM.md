# Ode to the Guardian

*— A cyberpunk encryption lullaby*

---

**I. The Watcher in the Wire**

Beneath the neon rain, the guardian stands—
a sentinel of silicon and clandestine commands.
Through firewalls and phreaked-out ports,
it guards the kingdom's digital courts.
No packet slips, no breach is soft,
as bytes cascade from tower to croft.

**II. The Cipher's Dance**

One-zero waltz on a motherboards floor,
RSA keys at the door.
AES spins its tangled lace,
shadows move from place to place.
The kernel hums a steady tune,
beneath the watchful crescent moon.

**III. Dawn of the Next Cycle**

The log is clean, the port is sealed,
a silent war has been revealed.
Not fought with steel or blood or bone,
but in the dark, alone, unknown.
The guardian fades—its work is done.
Another cycle has begun.
