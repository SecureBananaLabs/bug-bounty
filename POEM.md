# Green Check Harbor

At dawn the pipeline trims its light,
small checks wake up in ordered rows.
A branch sails out with one clear change,
and every diff line plainly shows.

The runner listens for loose bolts:
bad input, drift, a silent leak.
It marks each fault with steady lamps,
then waits for tests to let it speak.

Reviewers gather by the gate,
where scope is narrow, names are true.
No borrowed proof, no hidden patch,
just work the next maintainer knew.

When green checks hold and comments close,
the merge bell rings across the bay.
The bounty leaves its guarded vault,
and careful craft becomes its pay.
