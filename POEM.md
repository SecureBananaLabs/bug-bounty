# Changelog for a Trusted Handoff

## 0.1.0 - Brief

A client pins a need to the morning board,
plain as a ticket, small as a spark.
The scope is trimmed until every word
can find its edge in the dark.

## 0.2.0 - Proof

A maker answers with tests in their hands,
not promises polished for show.
Each claim is a path the reviewer can stand on,
each diff a place the facts can go.

## 0.3.0 - Review

The room grows quiet around the change,
comments folding risk into light.
Nothing is trusted because it is loud;
it is trusted because it survives sight.

## 0.4.0 - Merge

Green checks settle like stamps on a form,
the branch gives way to the line.
What was a proposal becomes part of the work,
versioned, named, and signed.

## 1.0.0 - Payout

Escrow releases without a speech,
just a ledger made clean and clear.
The handoff closes, the record remains:
proof paid for, and value here.
