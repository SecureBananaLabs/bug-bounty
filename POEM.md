# The Bounty Hunter's Creed

They said the system was airtight, sealed,
that no one could slip through the gate —
but gates have hinges, hinges have gaps,
and I've learned patience is the bait.

I mapped their surface like a cartographer
tracing fault lines before the quake:
the login form that whispered too much,
the token left to rot in plain-text lake.

---

Not malice — craft. The difference matters.
I file the report, I don't pull the thread.
A CVE is born from careful notes,
not from the chaos of systems bled.

SecureBananaLabs taught me this rhythm:
disclose, don't destroy; observe, don't own.
The bounty isn't money — it's the proof
that someone left a crack exposed in stone.

---

Every `eval()` is a door ajar,
every unchecked input, a held breath.
I read your source code like a love letter
written carelessly, flirting with death.

So here is my gift: the path I walked,
the payload that nearly slipped through clean.
Patch it, version it, close the loop —
and thank the eyes that haunt your attack scene.
