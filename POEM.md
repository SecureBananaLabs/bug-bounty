# POEM — FreelanceFlow

### The Ledger in the Clouds

A cursor blinks across an empty form,  
A job post waiting for its destined hand.  
The market stirs with every digital storm,  
As talents scatter across a cyber-land.  
One crafts a pipeline, one designs a screen,  
One writes the words that make the features known.  
The ledger grows in columns yet unseen,  
Each gig a brick in towers we have grown.

On Freelancer's search the profiles softly gleam,  
"Next.js, TypeScript, Figma, UX flow."  
A client searches, building out a dream,  
While both must trust what neither yet can know.  
The dashboard counts the bids, the milestones, pay—  
A reputation staked on work today.

Three stanzas flow like proposals in a queue,  
Each line a small deliverable come due.  
A contract sealed not by a handshake true  
But by a button click when both are through.  
The dispute queue waits silent in the wings,  
A moderation space for broken things.  
Yet most resolve—the escrow finds its mark,  
And freelancers light up a matching spark.

So flows the engine, pulse of gig and trade,  
Where code connects the offer to the made.  
No marble halls, no counting-house of old—  
A server farm of transactions, bought and sold.  
This is the flow: a freelance paradigm,  
And every PR helps build the paradigm.
