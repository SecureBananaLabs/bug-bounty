# The Bug Hunter's Code

In silent depths of darkened code I tread,
Where logic folds and edges fray unseen.
A single semicolon left for dead,
A null dereference where checks had been.
The stack trace whispers secrets in the gloom,
Each frame a clue on paths the data traced.
The payload waits within a hidden room
Where validation sleeps and bounds are graced.

The dashboard lights its amber warning glow,
A race condition caught in flight too late.
The logs unspool like ribbons as they flow,
A tale of sync and state and tangled fate.
The bounty waits for those who dare to pry,
Who hold the thread before the whole thing falls.
A silent pulse, a query's quiet sigh—
The weakest link becomes the call that calls.

The patch is written, tested, clean, and lean,
A single diff that makes the fortress whole.
No fanfare marks the closing of the scene,
No medal earned—just credit for the soul.
But in the quiet hours when systems sleep,
The hunter knows what others never see:
The bugs we catch are promises we keep
To keep the digital horizon free.
