# Acceptance Window

The brief arrives with numbered light,
a scope made small enough to hold;
we pin the edge, compare the night,
and name the work before it is sold.

The builder sends a measured proof,
not smoke, not hope, but steps that run;
each test becomes a quiet roof
above the risk we pulled from sun.

The reviewer reads the changed lines twice,
then checks the claim against the ground;
where evidence is clear and precise,
trust leaves a sharper, cleaner sound.

The merge gate opens without haste,
a record kept for all to see;
the payout moves with none to waste,
and craft becomes accountability.

So FreelanceFlow keeps work in view:
brief, proof, review, accepted pay;
small honest signals, passing through,
turn scattered effort into day.
