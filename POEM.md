# Clear Ledger

The brief arrives as measured light,
a scope line drawn without disguise;
each promise gets a place to stand,
each change a reason it can show.

Escrow waits behind the work,
quiet as a balanced sheet;
not trust alone, not hope alone,
but proof that both sides chose to keep.

The tests run through the market floor,
small signals turning red or green;
a failure names the missing edge,
a pass makes hidden labor seen.

Reviewers read the careful diff,
where claims are weighed against the code;
the merge is not a finish line,
but a record honestly owed.

Then payout follows verified work,
not thunder, luck, or hurried fame;
a clear ledger holds the path,
and every handoff keeps its name.
