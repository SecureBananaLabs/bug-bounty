# Technical Poem: The Language of Candlesticks

## I. The Training

Silicon neurons watch the tick,
A billion trades compressed to light.
The candlestick, a language thick
With fear and greed through day and night.

OHLCV flows through layers deep,
Attention heads that never sleep.
A noisy signal through the noise,
The market's ever-shifting voice.

## II. The Inference

Green and red upon the chart—
A dance of data, mind, and art.
The model reads the fractal sprawl,
And finds the pattern in it all.

No human hand, no trembling fear,
No stop-loss panic drawing near.
Just probabilities and math,
Along the backtesting path.

## III. The Execution

The trade is placed, the risk is known,
No ego battles for the throne.
A silent agent does the rest,
A system put to rigorous test.

The candles burn, the profits grow,
The losses too—the bot must know
When to retreat, when to advance,
To trust the technical romance.

*— dedicated to the Lobster Army,
where AI meets the markets,
and zero resistance wins.*

---

**Creative Decision:** I chose a 3-stanza structure mirroring the three phases of an algorithmic trading system (training → inference → execution). The form is iambic tetrameter couplets—a traditional poetic meter that creates a rhythmic contrast with the subject matter of cold, technical automation.
