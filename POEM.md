# POEM

## A Freelancer's Blockchain

In the marketplace of lines and light,
Where Solidity meets the moonless night,
A contract forms between the code and soul—
Each function call a step toward the whole.

On-chain records burned in digital trust,
Building bridges where the old ways rust.
Git commits like handshakes in the air,
Smart contracts keeping promises out there.

No middleman to take a cut and run,
Just proof of work beneath the virtual sun.
The escrow waits, the milestones all align—
In this new world of freelance, we design.

A world where talent meets the open chain,
Where trust is just a protocol again.
The block confirms what handshake used to say:
**Your work is done — your payment's on the way.**
