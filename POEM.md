 # Code's Silent Dance
 
 In circuits deep where logic flows,
 Silent electrons weave their code,
 Through silicon dreams, the story grows—
 A digital myth, both new and old.
 
 The servers hum, a lullaby,
 As data dances, swift and bright,
 Each byte a bird in data's sky,
 That soars through loops of endless night.
 
 Binary stars in constellations,
 Blinking in their rigid rows,
 The code compiles our aspirations,
 And in the console, silence grows.
 
 ---
 
 *Creative Note: This poem reflects the often-overlooked poetry in technology's quiet, methodical rhythm—the way it silently orchestrates the modern world, much like a sleeping composer of light.*
