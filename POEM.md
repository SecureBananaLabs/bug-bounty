# The Marketplace

## I. Listings

In the quiet hum of servers overnight,
a thousand hopes are posted, priced, and pinned —
each job a door left open to the light,
each bid a voice cast outward to the wind.

## II. The Handshake

No office, no commute, no corner clock —
just token, timestamp, handshake, and a name.
Two strangers meet where code and payment lock;
the contract seals; the work begins the same.

## III. Delivery

The final push at three a.m. comes through:
reviewed, approved, the escrow clicks to paid.
What started as a brief becomes the view
through someone else's window, newly made.
