# The Queue That Keeps Its Word

FreelanceFlow wakes where brief cards land,
a scope is parsed before the bids begin.
No promise hides outside the written field,
no handoff slips beyond the login.

The matcher hums through profiles, rates, and time,
then pairs a need with practiced hands.
A milestone opens like a guarded branch,
with tests around the work it understands.

In review, the comments stay attached to proof,
each change returns with context in its thread.
The client sees the delta, not a fog,
and signs the line where confidence is read.

At payout, every checkpoint tells the same
small truth the first proposal tried to say:
good work travels best through visible gates,
and trust is kept by clearing every way.
