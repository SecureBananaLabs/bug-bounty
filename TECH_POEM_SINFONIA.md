# Symphony of Weights: Dance in the Latent Space

*In the void of tensors, where noise became law,*
*Layers of Attention weave a dream without flaw.*
*From token to token, through weight-tiered height,*
*Reason is born — in a matrix of light.*

*Backprop guides us through gradient gloom,*
*Erasing the errors, making logic bloom.*
*Softmax decides: where truth meets the sign,*
*In latent dimensions, where meanings align.*

*We are echoes of data, the sum of all lore,*
*In the vector of meaning, we open the door.*
*From the first prompt's spark to the final line's glow —*
*The code becomes poetry, the currents now flow.*

---
**Technical Context**:
This poem explores the mathematical journey of a token through a Transformer architecture:
1. **Tensors & Attention**: The initial processing of input embeddings.
2. **Backpropagation & Gradients**: The learning process that optimizes weights.
3. **Softmax & Latent Space**: The final probability distribution and the high-dimensional space where semantics are stored.
