I comb the codebase for a hairline sign,
a quiet fracture no scanner has found,
the instant logic forgot to hold the line.

I trace the calls that tunnel underground,
one bad assumption leaving no clean trace,
until the missing guardrail can be found.

I write it up and give the bug a face,
a scope, a name, a timeline made aware,
so what was silent leaves a public trace.

They thank the finder, greenlight the repair,
a patch rolls out to stop the harm again,
the system breathes, a little more aware,
though somewhere, one more crack will still remain.
