# The Ballad of the Late Night Coder

The office lights are burning low,
The coffee's cold, the hour's late,
Another bug he's come to know,
He types and curses at his fate.

The feature seemed so simple once,
A checkbox and a button there,
But now the code's a tangled mess,
A labyrinth of deep despair.

At 3 AM the answer comes,
A single line he had forgot,
He adds it in, the program runs,
The bug is gone, it's now a blot.

He pushes, pulls, creates the PR,
And heads to bed beneath the stars,
The code works now, it's gone so far,
Tomorrow he'll fix similar scars.
