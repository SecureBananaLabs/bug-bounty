# A Poem for the Bug Bounty

## "The Silent Guardian"

```
In lines of code where shadows fall,
A silent guardian hears the call.
Through stacks of logs and endless night,
It hunts for bugs with all its might.

The tokens flow, the servers hum,
A single voice cries, "This is done!"
But in the depths where logic breaks,
A hidden flaw its silence makes.

No sleep for those who seek the flaw,
Who trace the edge of every law.
Of syntax strict and runtime grace,
They find the cracks in time and space.

Yet in that space, a world is born,
Where broken things are patched and torn.
Made whole again by careful hands,
That understand the code's commands.

So raise a glass to those who see,
The ghosts that live in memory.
Who leak no byte, who crash no state,
Who keep the digital world sedate.

For every bug that meets its end,
A silent victory, a quiet friend.
The code grows strong, the system thrives,
Where once a thousand bugs had lives.
```

— Freeman88-tch, 2026
For the SecureBananaLabs Bug Bounty
